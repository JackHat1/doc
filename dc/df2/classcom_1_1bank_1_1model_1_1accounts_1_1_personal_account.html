Detailed Description

Represents a personal bank account that can have multiple co-owners.

The PersonalAccount extends Account to support shared ownership between multiple Individual users. Each account has one primary owner (from the superclass) and zero or more co-owners.

Serialization:

The marshal() method extends the base account format by including coOwner:<VAT> entries for each co-owner. When deserializing, unmarshal(String) extracts these VATs into coOwnerVats, which can later be resolved into Individual objects.
