Detailed Description

Represents a payable bill/invoice with issuer, customer, amount and due date.

A Bill can be serialized to a compact key-value line using marshal() and restored with unmarshal(String). The relational links are stored using VAT fields:

  • issuerVat (VAT of the issuer account's owner) and
  • customerVat (VAT of the customer)

The concrete Account reference for the issuer must be resolved externally (e.g., via a repository) and assigned via setIssuer(Account) after loading.
