Detailed Description

CLI interface for administrators to inspect users, accounts, bills and standing orders.

Provides read/maintenance operations:

  • List users and show customer details
  • List bank accounts and inspect specific account info/statements
  • List issued/paid bills and load company bills
  • List/execute standing orders
  • Simulate time (interest accrual and fees)

Dependencies:

Uses UserManager, AccountManager, BillManager, TransactionManager, StandingOrderManager and a shared Scanner.
