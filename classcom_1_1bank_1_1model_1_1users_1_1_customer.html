Detailed Description

Abstract base class representing a general bank customer.

The Customer class extends User and adds a unique VAT (Tax Identification Number, ΑΦΜ) used to identify individuals and companies within the banking system.

Purpose:

  • Provides a common structure for Individual and Company users.
  • Ensures every customer type includes a VAT number for billing and tax purposes.
