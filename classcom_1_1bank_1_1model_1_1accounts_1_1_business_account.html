Detailed Description

Represents a business (company) bank account with an additional monthly maintenance fee.

The BusinessAccount class extends Account to model accounts belonging to companies rather than individual customers. In addition to the standard account properties (balance, interest rate, etc.), it includes a monthly fee field representing account maintenance costs.

Serialization:

The marshal() and unmarshal(String) methods extend the base serialization format to include the fee field.
