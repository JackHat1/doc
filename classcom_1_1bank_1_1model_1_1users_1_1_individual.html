Detailed Description

Represents an individual (personal) customer in the banking system.

The Individual class extends Customer and models a private person using the banking services. Each individual has a unique VAT (ΑΦΜ), login credentials, and can own com.bank.model.accounts.PersonalAccount instances, perform transactions, and manage bills.

Key Characteristics:

  • Inherits authentication and identification fields from Customer.
  • Fixed role: "Individual".
  • Serializable via marshal() and unmarshal(String).
