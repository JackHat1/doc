Detailed Description

Represents a business customer of the banking system.

The Company class extends Customer to represent corporate or organizational users with a registered VAT number. A company can own com.bank.model.accounts.BusinessAccount instances and issue com.bank.model.bills.Bills to other customers.

Key Characteristics:

  • Inherits login and identity information from Customer.
  • Includes a unique VAT number (vat).
  • Role is fixed as "Company".
