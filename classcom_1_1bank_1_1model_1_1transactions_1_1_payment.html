Detailed Description

Represents a bill payment transaction between a customer and a business account.

The Payment class extends Transaction and encapsulates the logic for paying a bill. It withdraws funds from the payer’s account, deposits them into the business account, and marks the associated Bill as paid.

Execution flow:

  1. Check if the payer account has enough balance.
  2. Withdraw the bill amount from the payer.
  3. Deposit the bill amount into the business account.
  4. Mark the Bill as paid.
  5. Create and save corresponding StatementEntry objects for both accounts.
Note
  • The payment is skipped if the balance is insufficient.
  • Both debit (payer) and credit (business) sides are logged.

◆ execute()

void com.bank.model.transactions.Payment.execute ( )

Executes the payment transaction.

Withdraws the bill amount from the payer account, deposits it into the business account, and marks the bill as paid. Two StatementEntry records are created:

  • Debit entry for the payer.
  • Credit entry for the business.

If insufficient funds exist, the payment is not executed.

Console Output Example:

Payment of 50.0€ to business GR2009988776655 [RF: RF12345]
