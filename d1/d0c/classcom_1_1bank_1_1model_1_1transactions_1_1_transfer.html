Detailed Description

Represents a money transfer between two bank accounts.

The Transfer class extends Transaction and performs a fund transfer between two Account objects. It debits the sender’s account and credits the receiver’s account by the same amount.

Execution Flow:

  1. Verify that the sender’s balance is sufficient.
  2. Withdraw funds from the sender and deposit into the receiver.
  3. Create two StatementEntry objects:
    • Debit entry for the sender.
    • Credit entry for the receiver.
  4. Persist both statements using StatementManager#saveStatement(Account, StatementEntry).
Note
  • If the sender does not have enough balance, the transfer is aborted.
  • Both sender and receiver transactions are logged in their respective statements.

◆ execute()

void com.bank.model.transactions.Transfer.execute ( )

Executes the transfer transaction.

If sufficient balance exists in the sender’s account:

  • Withdraws amount from from.
  • Deposits the same amount into to.
  • Logs both sides of the transaction with StatementEntrys.

Otherwise, prints a message and aborts execution.

Console Output Example:

Transfer of 250.0€ from GR100111222333 to GR100444555666
Sender Reason: Rent payment
Receiver Reason: Rent income
